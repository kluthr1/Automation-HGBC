Finance & Investment 101

Course Description: The Finance & Investment 101 Series is a newly launched program by the Harvard Graduate Business Club, designed for graduate students who are seeking a degree in a non-business related field. This series aims to equip students with a solid foundation and toolkit in the area of Finance, enabling them to consider and opt for a career in the financial business world.

This initiative is structured to cover topics on (i) Personal Finance (Retail Investing, Portfolio Balancing, Real Estate Investments, etc.) and on (ii) Careers in Finance (Investment Banking, Corporate Finance, Principal Investments, Startup Finance, Interviewing for Wall Street, etc.) through a series of introductory seminars and interactive workshops. Whether you are new to the world of Finance or you are already considering breaking into Wall Street, watch this space!
