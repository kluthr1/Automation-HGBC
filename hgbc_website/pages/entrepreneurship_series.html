Entreprenuership Series

Description: This entrepreneurship panel series aims to offer insight from the experts into the challenging journey of company creation. Each month, we hold themed panel discussions centered around specific stages of venture creation, and is open to all aspiring student entrepreneurs. Join us as we answer questions like:

  • How can you tell if the idea is good?
  • What are the most important first steps?
  • What resources are available?
