Venture Capital

Description: The HGBC venture capital team aims to bridge the gap between the wealth of science and technology in academia and the resources of the business world. Our programs include a variety of lectures, panel discussions, site visits and networking events designed to allow our members to see things from the investors' perspective during the process of commercializing promising technology.
