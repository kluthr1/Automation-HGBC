Mission

Harvard GSAS Business Club is a dedicated association that aspires to build and nourish a vibrant and connected community of graduate students, who are interested in exploring career opportunities in the business world. We offer a range of events and workshops around career development, networking and business education, enabling graduate students to establish a foothold and achieve their goals in the business world.

Career opportunities: Our career panels and site visits cover topics such as Investment Banking, Private Equity, Venture Capital and Entrepreneurship. Learn how your PhD training and scientific background can be applied into different business and industry context! We enable you to unleash your full potential by considering career opportunities outside your own research focus area.

Networking: Have you ever pictured making a name for yourself on Wall Street or becoming an Entrepreneur? You are not alone. Join our networking events where you can meet like-minded peers and learn from MBA students at our Case Study Journal Club. We will connect you with your future business partners.
