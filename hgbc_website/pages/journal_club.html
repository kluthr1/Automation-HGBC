Business Case Journal Club

Description: The Business Case Journal Club is designed in collaboration with the Doctoral of Business Administration (DBA) program at Harvard Business School, and represents an opportunity for non-business graduate students to gather and learn business principles using the renowned MBA case method. Each month, we invite a DBA student to select a teaching case centered around one or more topics of the MBA curriculum and lead its discussion in a group of 30-50 people at the HBS or main campus. Participants are expected to read the selected case in detail before the journal club and to actively participate in the discussions.
