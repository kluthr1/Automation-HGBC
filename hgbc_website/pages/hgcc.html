Harvard Global Case Competition

Visit Competition Home Page

Description: The Harvard Graduate Business Club is proud to be hosting the prestigious Harvard Global Case Competition 2020 in collaboration with the International Finance Students Association (IFSA) Network and the European Business Group (EBG)!

Each year, the Harvard Global Case Competition brings together the best and the brightest minds in the world to compete at Harvard on a finance-related case. Since its inception in 2016, the competition has attracted 338 teams (1700 students) across the world to compete for a chance to win $10,000. Teams are given 3 weeks to prepare and submit a solution, and the top 10 teams will be invited to Harvard University in April 2020 for a series of networking events, and to pitch their solutions to a panel of judges comprising of distinguished business executives and Harvard Business School professors. Winners of the $10,000 grand prize will be announced at a black-tie gala the same evening.

Participants get the once-in-a-lifetime chance to receive a world-class learning experience through a hands-on business case, and to build a unique network with students from all over the world and with executives from the worldâ€™s leading institutions.
